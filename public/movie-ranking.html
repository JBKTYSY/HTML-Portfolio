The Best Movies According to Jadzia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Miracle In Cell No.7 (7번방의 선물)

Released in 2013.

This is a South Korean movie about a father with an intellectual disability who is coerced into pleading guilty by a police commander,
of sexually assaulting and murdering a little girl, who is the daughter of the commander.
This film is telling the story of a father who is falsely accused, who's daughter legally clears his name when she grows up.
It's a sad, heart-wrenching story based on a true events.

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Released in 2022.

This is an asian-american comedic sci-fi movie about jumping through different dimensional realities.
This film also explores the struggles of immigrants from the perspective of a chinese family and generational trauma yet is also heartwarming and emotional.

Better Days (少年的你)

Released in 2019.

This is a movie about a school girl who is suffering through severe bullying, she meets another teen who is a street thug and he becomes her protector.
The plot escalates after the schoolgirl accidentally kills her bully and her protector trying to take the blame for her.
This movie hints at the different lives that people are living in the world and how they are treated due to the social norms.
